🏷️ What Are Labels & Stereotypes?

Labels are names people use to define you. Stereotypes are oversimplified ideas about people. Both can affect how we see ourselves.

  • 📚 “Nerd”
  • ⚽ “Jock”
  • 🎭 “Dramatic”
  • 🙃 “Weird”
  • 💡 “Smart one”
“Don’t let anyone define you with their words. You are more than a label.”
