💪 My Strengths & Weaknesses

🌟 Self-Esteem & Identity Workbook

📘 What This Workbook Is About

Understanding your strengths and weaknesses helps you build true confidence. 💡 This workbook helps you reflect on both — without judgment.

🌈 What Makes Me Strong?

List some of your personal strengths. These can be skills, character traits, or ways you support others.

🧩 My Weaknesses — Without Shame

We all have areas to grow. Write a few things you’re working on or find difficult right now.

🪞 How Do My Strengths Help Me?

Pick 2 strengths and explain how they help you in daily life or challenges.

⚡ Turning Weaknesses Into Growth

What’s one small way you can improve or work around a weakness this week?

🧠 What Others Say About Me

Think about a compliment or positive feedback you’ve received. What did it reveal about your strengths?

💚 I Am Not My Weaknesses

Write a sentence to remind yourself that your weaknesses don’t define you:

🌟 Strength Affirmation

Finish this sentence: “One thing I love about myself is...”
