🧠 Identity Workbook

🧬🌱 Nature vs. Nurture – What Shaped Me?

🌟 What You'll Explore

  • 🧬 Learn what "nature vs. nurture" really means
  • 🌱 Reflect on what shaped your personality and beliefs
  • 🔎 Discover what makes you *you*

🧬 Nature: My Genetic Traits

Think about things you may have inherited—like your personality, talents, or appearance.

🌱 Nurture: My Life Experiences

What events, people, or environments have helped shape you?

🏡 Family & Upbringing

How has your family shaped your habits, beliefs, or confidence?

👥 Friends & Social Circles

Have your friends changed how you see yourself?

📺 Culture, Media & Role Models

What media or people outside your home influenced your values or interests?

🧭 My Core Beliefs – Where Did They Come From?

Write about a belief you hold and explore whether it came from nature, nurture, or both.

🪞 Me Today – A Mix of Both

In your own words, describe how nature and nurture both shape who you are today.

💡 Reflect & Affirm

Write an affirmation that honors your unique mix of nature and nurture:

“Genes load the gun, environment pulls the trigger.” – Francis Collins
