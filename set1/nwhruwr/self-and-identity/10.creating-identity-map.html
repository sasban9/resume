🗺️ Creating My Identity Map

🌟 What You'll Explore

  • 💭 Who you are at your core
  • 🎨 The things that shape you
  • 🧩 How your traits, values, and dreams connect

🔍 What is an Identity Map?

An identity map helps you see the different *parts* that make up who you are. It's like a mind map of your personality, interests, beliefs, and experiences.

“Knowing yourself is the beginning of all wisdom.” — Aristotle

👤 Core Values

What are the 3–5 values that matter most to you? (e.g., honesty, kindness, courage)

💬 Words That Describe Me

List 5 words you believe describe your personality.

🌍 My Background

Where are you from? What culture or community do you feel a part of?

🎯 My Interests & Passions

What activities or subjects make you feel most alive?

🎭 Roles I Play

What roles do you play in your life? (e.g., student, friend, sibling, artist, helper...)

💪 Strengths I See in Myself

What are your personal strengths and talents?

😣 Challenges I've Faced

What difficult experiences have helped shape who you are?

🌱 Who I’m Becoming

How do you want to grow? What kind of person are you becoming?

✨ My Identity Map Summary

Write a paragraph that brings everything together. Describe yourself like you're writing your "about me" bio!
