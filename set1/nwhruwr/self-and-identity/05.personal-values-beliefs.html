💫 Self-Esteem & Identity: My Personal Values and Beliefs

✨ What You'll Learn

  • 🌟 Understand the role of values in shaping identity
  • 💭 Reflect on what really matters to you
  • 🧭 Use your beliefs to make confident decisions

🧠 What Are Personal Values?

Personal values are your guiding principles. They help you decide what’s right, what matters, and who you want to be.

Examples: honesty, kindness, respect, creativity, courage, fairness, freedom.

🔍 What Do I Value Most?

Pick 5 values from the list above or your own that feel most important to who you are.

❤️ Why Do These Values Matter to Me?

For each value, write a short reason why it’s important to you.

🧭 How My Beliefs Guide Me

Describe a time when one of your values helped you make a decision or handle a situation.

🌀 Are My Values Really Mine?

Sometimes we absorb beliefs from family, culture, or school. Reflect: Which of your values feel truly *yours*?

🛤️ Living by My Values

What’s one small action you can take this week that shows your values in real life?

🌈 My Core Belief Statement

Create a personal mantra that reflects your values and how you want to live.
