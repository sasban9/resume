🎭 What Are Social Masks?

Sometimes we act a certain way around others to fit in or protect ourselves. These are our “masks.”

  • 😊 A smile you wear even when you're sad
  • 💬 Talking a lot to hide nervousness
  • 😎 Pretending to be confident when unsure
“We all wear masks, and the time comes when we cannot remove them without removing some of our own skin.” — André Berthiaume
