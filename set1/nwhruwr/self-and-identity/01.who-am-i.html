💭 What Does “Identity” Mean?

Your identity is the story you tell yourself about who you are. It includes:

  • 💡 Your values and beliefs
  • 🎯 What you care about most
  • 🎭 Your personality and unique traits
  • 🌍 Your culture, background, and community
"Be yourself—everyone else is already taken." — Oscar Wilde
