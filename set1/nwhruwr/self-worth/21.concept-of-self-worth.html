💖 Self-Worth Workbook

🌟 Understanding the Concept of Self-Worth

🔍 What is Self-Worth?

Your self-worth is the belief that you are valuable and deserving of love and respect – just as you are. It's not based on achievements, appearance, or how others treat you.

“You are worthy. Not because of what you do, but because of who you are.”

🧠 What Do You Believe About Yourself?

Write down 3 beliefs you currently have about your self-worth (be honest).

💡 Where Did These Beliefs Come From?

Think about where you picked up these beliefs — from family, school, social media, etc.

💬 Rewriting the Story

Now, turn one negative belief into a new positive one.

🪞 Self-Worth in Action

List 5 things you deserve *just because you're you*.

🌻 Accepting Yourself

Write down something you usually criticize yourself for. Now, write a kind message in response.

🌈 My Worth is Constant

Even when things go wrong, your worth stays the same. What’s one reminder you can tell yourself?

💖 Weekly Reflection

Each day this week, write one reason you are proud of simply being you.
