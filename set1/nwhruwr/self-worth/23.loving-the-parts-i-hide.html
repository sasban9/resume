🌟 Self-Worth Workbook

💛 Loving the Parts I Usually Hide

🔍 What We'll Explore

  • 🧠 Why we hide certain parts of ourselves
  • 💖 Learning to be gentle with our imperfections
  • 🌈 Accepting our full, real, beautiful selves

🫣 What Do I Hide?

Think about the sides of yourself you tend to keep hidden. These could be feelings, thoughts, habits, quirks, or insecurities.

🧱 Where Did That Come From?

Can you remember when or why you started hiding these parts?

❤️ What’s the Truth?

Now flip the story. What's the truth about these parts? Could they be signs of strength or depth?

🪞 Talking to My Hidden Self

If you could speak to the parts of you that feel hidden, what would you say?

🌱 Tiny Steps to Self-Love

List 3 small ways you can begin to show love to these hidden parts this week.

💬 Self-Acceptance Affirmation

Write your own self-acceptance affirmation.

🌟 Challenge of the Week

Do one brave thing that lets a hidden part of you be seen (even a little bit!).

"You don't have to be perfect to be lovable. You just have to be real." 💛
