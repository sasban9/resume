🧠💬 My Inner Critic – Recognizing Negative Self-Talk

🔍 What Is the Inner Critic?

We all have an inner voice — sometimes it cheers us on, and sometimes it criticizes. The "Inner Critic" is the voice that puts us down or makes us doubt ourselves.

  • 🗯️ "I’m not good enough."
  • 😟 "I always mess things up."
  • 🚫 "Nobody likes me."
“You talk to yourself more than anyone else. Make sure you're saying the right things.” 💡

🎧 What Does My Inner Critic Say?

Write down some of the negative things your inner critic says to you.

🧩 Where Does It Come From?

Think about where these thoughts might come from — experiences, people, or beliefs?

🚦How Does It Make Me Feel?

Notice the feelings your inner critic brings up in you.

💭 Flip the Script

Take one negative thought and rewrite it into a kind, supportive message.

👂 What Would a Friend Say?

If your best friend heard you say that negative thought, what kind and encouraging words would they say to you?

🛠️ My Positive Reframe Toolbox

Create a list of kind reminders or mantras to use when your inner critic shows up.
