🤔 What Is Self-Doubt?

Self-doubt is the inner voice that tells you you're not good enough, smart enough, or strong enough. It's not the truth — it's just fear dressed up as "facts."

"You have been criticizing yourself for years, and it hasn’t worked. Try approving of yourself and see what happens." — Louise Hay
