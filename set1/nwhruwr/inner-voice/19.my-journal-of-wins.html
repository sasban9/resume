🏆 My Journal of Wins

🪞 Self Esteem & Identity: Daily/Weekly Reflections

✨ Why This Journal?

Confidence grows with small wins. This journal helps you notice your strengths, celebrate effort, and build a healthy sense of identity. 🌱

🔍 What Counts as a "Win"?

  • 🌞 Getting out of bed on a tough day
  • 🧠 Learning something new
  • 💬 Speaking up in class or group
  • 🎨 Finishing a creative project
  • ❤️ Helping someone or showing kindness
A win is anything that moved you forward, no matter how small.
