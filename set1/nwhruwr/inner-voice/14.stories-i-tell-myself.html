💭 Self-Esteem & Identity Workbook

📖 The Stories I Tell Myself

🧠 What You’ll Explore

  • 📝 Understand the inner stories you repeat about yourself
  • 🔍 Identify which ones help and which ones hurt
  • 💬 Learn to rewrite limiting stories into empowering ones

📚 What Are Inner Stories?

We all tell ourselves stories about who we are. Some come from experiences, others from what people have told us. Over time, they shape how we see ourselves. Let’s explore yours!

“The most important story you’ll ever tell is the one you tell yourself.”

🧾 A Story I Often Tell Myself

What’s a sentence or belief you often repeat about yourself (good or bad)?

🔄 Where Did That Story Come From?

Was it something someone said? An experience? Try to trace its origin.

❓Is It 100% True?

Be honest with yourself. Is this story always true — or only sometimes?

🧠 How This Story Affects Me

When you believe this story, how do you feel or act?

💪 A Story I Want to Start Telling Myself

If you could choose a new story that lifts you up, what would it be?

🛠️ How Can I Reinforce This New Story?

Think of small actions, affirmations, or reminders to support your new story.

✨ My Empowering Self-Story

Write your new story as if it’s already true. Let it shine!
