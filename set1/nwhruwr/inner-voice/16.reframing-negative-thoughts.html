🌟 What You'll Learn

  • 🔍 Identify negative self-talk
  • 🧠 Understand how thoughts impact feelings
  • 🔄 Practice turning critical thoughts into kinder ones

🧠 What is Negative Self-Talk?

It's the voice in your head that says things like:

  • “I’m not good enough.” 😞
  • “Everyone else is better than me.” 😔
  • “I always mess things up.” 😢
“Don’t believe everything you think.” — Unknown

🪞 My Negative Thought

Write a negative thought you’ve had about yourself recently.

📖 What's the Story Behind This Thought?

Where do you think this thought comes from? Is it based on facts or fears?

🔁 What Would I Say to a Friend?

If a friend said this about themselves, how would you respond?

💡 Reframe That Thought

Now turn the negative thought into a helpful or realistic one.

🎯 What I’ll Try Next Time

What action or mindset shift will you take the next time this thought appears?
