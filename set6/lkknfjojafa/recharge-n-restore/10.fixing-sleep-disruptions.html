🌙 Sleep Wellness Workbook

💤 Fixing Sleep Disruptions (Overthinking, Nightmares, etc.)

🛏️ What You'll Learn

  • 😴 Understanding common causes of sleep disruptions
  • 🧠 How overthinking impacts sleep and ways to calm your mind
  • 🌜 Techniques to reduce nightmares and get better rest

💭 What’s On Your Mind?

Overthinking at night is a common reason for not getting quality sleep. Write down the thoughts that tend to keep you awake.

🧘 Relaxation Techniques

List some relaxation techniques that you think could help calm your mind before bed.

🌙 Nightmares

Nightmares can be scary and disruptive to your sleep. What nightmares have you experienced? Write about them here.

🌱 What Makes Me Feel Safe at Night?

Think about what helps you feel safe and calm at night, whether it’s physical comfort or mental reassurance.

💪 Sleep Hygiene Tips

Write down ways to improve your sleep hygiene, such as habits or changes that can help you sleep better at night.

🍏 Diet & Sleep

Food and drinks can impact your sleep. List any foods or drinks that might be affecting your sleep, and think of some alternatives.

💪 Physical Wellness & Sleep

Physical activity can help with better sleep. How does your physical activity affect your sleep? Do you notice any patterns?

🧘 Evening Wind-Down Routine

Write out a calming wind-down routine that you can do before bed to relax your mind and body.

💬 Self-Talk for Sleep

What positive self-talk can you say to yourself to ease worries before bed?

📅 Sleep Tracking

Keep track of your sleep for the next week. What time do you go to bed? When do you wake up? How do you feel when you wake up?
