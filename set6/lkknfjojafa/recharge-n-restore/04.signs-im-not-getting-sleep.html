😴 The Power of Sleep

Sleep is crucial for your physical and mental health. But sometimes, we don’t realize how much it's affecting us until it's too late!

“Sleep is the best meditation.” — Dalai Lama

🔎 Signs I’m Not Getting Enough Sleep

Check off any of these signs you’ve experienced recently. Are you getting enough rest?

  • 😴 Feeling constantly tired or groggy, even after waking up
  • 💭 Trouble concentrating or focusing
  • 🍽️ Unexplained cravings for junk food
  • 😠 Feeling irritable or moody for no reason
  • 😣 Feeling anxious or stressed more often than usual
  • 😌 Low energy or motivation to do things
  • 🛌 Having trouble falling asleep or staying asleep

💭 How Sleep Affects My Mood

Think about how lack of sleep affects your mood and emotions. Are you more likely to snap at others or feel overwhelmed?

💡 Improving My Sleep Hygiene

Here are some steps you can take to improve your sleep quality:

  • 🕒 Set a regular bedtime and wake-up time.
  • 📵 Avoid screens (phone, laptop, etc.) for at least 30 minutes before bed.
  • 🌙 Create a calming bedtime routine (e.g., read, meditate, etc.).
  • 🥛 Avoid heavy meals or caffeine before bed.
  • 🏃‍♂️ Get daily physical activity, but avoid intense exercise too close to bedtime.
  • 🛏️ Make your sleep environment comfortable (cool, dark, and quiet).
