🌙 Sleep Journal

🍏 Physical Wellness - Tracking My Sleep, Diet & Patterns

🌟 What You'll Track

  • 🛏️ Your sleep patterns
  • 🍽️ Your daily diet
  • 💪 Your physical wellness habits

🕒 Sleep Schedule

Track your sleep time, wake-up time, and any interruptions during the night.

🌙 Sleep Quality Rating

On a scale from 1 to 10, rate the quality of your sleep.

🍏 Diet Check

List what you ate during the day, including meals and snacks. Pay attention to any foods or drinks that may affect your sleep.

💪 Physical Activity

Note your physical activity for the day. Did you exercise? How did it affect your energy levels or sleep?

🧘‍♀️ Relaxation Before Bed

Did you take any steps to relax before bed? Think about your activities like reading, stretching, or using screen time.

💡 Insights & Patterns

Reflect on how your sleep, diet, and physical wellness are related. Are there any patterns you notice?

🌱 Weekly Check-In

At the end of the week, review your journal. How did you feel overall? What improvements or changes can you make next week?
