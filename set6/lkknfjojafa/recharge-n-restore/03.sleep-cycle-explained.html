💡 Why Sleep Matters

  • 🧠 Helps your brain rest & recharge
  • 💪 Repairs muscles and boosts immunity
  • 🎯 Improves focus, memory, and mood

🔄 The 4 Stages of Sleep

There are 4 stages in your sleep cycle. Let's simplify them:

  • 😴 Stage 1: Light Sleep – the “drifting off” stage
  • 😌 Stage 2: Deeper Light Sleep – heart rate slows, body relaxes
  • 🛠️ Stage 3: Deep Sleep – your body does most of the healing
  • 🎥 Stage 4: REM Sleep – dreams happen, brain activity increases

⏱ How Long is a Sleep Cycle?

Each cycle lasts about 90 minutes. You go through 4–6 of them each night!
