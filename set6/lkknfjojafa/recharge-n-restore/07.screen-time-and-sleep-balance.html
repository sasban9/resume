📱 The Impact of Screen Time

Too much screen time, especially before bed, can interfere with your sleep cycle. The blue light from screens can make it harder for you to fall asleep.

“Avoid screens 30-60 minutes before bed for better sleep.” 🌙

🛏️ Sleep Hygiene Tips

Good sleep hygiene can help you get a better night’s rest:

  • 🚫 Avoid caffeine and heavy meals before bed
  • 📱 Create a screen-free zone at least 30 minutes before sleep
  • 💤 Try relaxing activities like reading or stretching
