🌿 Building a Sleep-Friendly Routine

💭 What You’ll Learn

  • 💤 Why sleep is crucial for wellness
  • 🥗 How diet affects your sleep
  • 🏃‍♀️ The role of physical activity in rest
  • 🛏️ Creating a peaceful nighttime routine

😴 Understanding Your Sleep Cycle

Think about your current sleep habits. How many hours do you sleep on average each night?

🍽️ Diet & Sleep

What foods do you eat in the evening? Some foods can help you sleep, while others can disrupt it.

🍎 What Could Help?

List some foods or drinks that you think could improve your sleep quality. Consider things like magnesium, herbal teas, and avoiding caffeine before bed.

🏋️‍♀️ Exercise & Sleep

How often do you exercise? Physical activity can help you fall asleep faster and sleep more soundly.

🛀 Building a Sleep Routine

Think about your nighttime routine. What steps can you take to wind down and prepare for sleep? You might try reading, stretching, or relaxing with soft music.

📱 Avoiding Distractions

How do electronic devices (like phones or computers) affect your ability to fall asleep? Try setting boundaries for screen time before bed.

🌙 Your Ideal Sleep Schedule

Think about what an ideal sleep schedule would look like for you. Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep each night.

💡 Simple Sleep Tips

Here are a few simple tips to improve your sleep quality:

  • 🛏️ Make your bedroom a peaceful space.
  • 🕯️ Set a consistent bedtime and wake-up time.
  • 📵 Limit screen exposure 30 minutes before bed.
  • 🍃 Try relaxation techniques, such as deep breathing.

📝 Wellness Challenge

For the next 7 days, keep track of your sleep, diet, and exercise habits.
