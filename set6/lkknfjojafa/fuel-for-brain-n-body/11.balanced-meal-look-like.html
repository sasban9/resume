🥦 What Makes a Balanced Meal?

A balanced meal includes a mix of protein, carbohydrates, fats, and essential nutrients. Let’s break it down:

  • 🍗 **Protein**: Essential for muscle growth and repair
  • 🥔 **Carbohydrates**: Provide energy for the day
  • 🥑 **Healthy Fats**: Keep your heart and brain healthy
  • 🍅 **Fruits & Vegetables**: Packed with vitamins and minerals
  • 💧 **Water**: Vital for hydration

🍽️ Create Your Own Balanced Meal

Think about your favorite foods. Which food groups do they fall into? Try to build a balanced meal using those items.

💡 Why Sleep Is Key to Wellness

Good sleep is just as important as eating healthy. Lack of sleep can lead to tiredness, irritability, and poor concentration.

“Sleep is the best meditation.” – Dalai Lama

How many hours of sleep do you get on average? How do you feel the next day?

🏃 Physical Activity & Wellness

Exercise is essential for overall health. Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight, boosts mood, and improves energy levels.

What type of physical activity do you enjoy? Write down a weekly plan for how you can stay active.

🥛 Nutrition & Hydration

We often forget how important it is to drink enough water. Staying hydrated helps with energy, digestion, and mental clarity.

Write down your daily water intake goal and how you’ll remind yourself to drink water throughout the day.

🌞 Your Personal Wellness Checklist

Use this checklist to create a wellness routine that includes sleep, diet, and physical activity:

  • ✅ Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep each night
  • ✅ Eat a balanced meal with protein, carbs, fats, and vegetables
  • ✅ Drink at least 8 glasses of water per day
  • ✅ Exercise 3-5 times per week (find something you enjoy!)

Which items on this checklist do you feel you’re already doing well? Which ones can you work on?

🌱 Weekly Wellness Challenge

Challenge yourself to make one change this week that will improve your sleep, diet, or physical wellness.
