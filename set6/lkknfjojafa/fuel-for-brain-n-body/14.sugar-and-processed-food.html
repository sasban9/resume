🧠🍕 Teen Wellness Workbook

🍭 Sugar and Processed Food: Know the Impact

🧐 What We'll Explore

  • 🍬 How sugar affects your brain, mood, and energy
  • 🥪 What “processed” really means in food
  • 🏃 Tips for feeling strong and balanced

🍰 What Did I Eat Today?

Make a quick list of what you ate or drank in the last 24 hours. Be honest!

🔍 Hidden Sugar Hunt

Pick 3 packaged foods you’ve eaten recently. Check the labels. How much sugar is there?

🤯 How Did I Feel After?

Think about your energy, mood, focus, or sleep after eating these things.

💪 Fuel That Works for Me

Write down a few foods or snacks that make you feel full, focused, and happy.

🚫 Processed Food Swap

Choose one processed food and list 2 healthier swaps you could try.

🌱 Challenge of the Week

Cut back on just ONE sugary or processed food this week. Track how you feel!

✨ Self-Care Affirmation

Write an affirmation to remind yourself that you deserve to feel good inside and out.

"You are what you eat — so choose food that makes you feel energized, not exhausted." 🥦💥
