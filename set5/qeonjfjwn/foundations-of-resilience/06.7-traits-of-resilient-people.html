The 7 Traits of Resilient People 🌈

🌟 What Does It Mean to Be Resilient?

Resilient people bounce back after challenges. They grow through what they go through.

1️⃣ Optimism – Hope for Better Days 🌞

Describe a time when staying hopeful helped you keep going.

2️⃣ Emotional Awareness – Naming Your Feelings 🎭

What emotions do you find hardest to recognize or express? Why?

3️⃣ Self-Efficacy – I Can Handle This! 🧠

Write about a challenge where you surprised yourself with your strength.

4️⃣ Connection – Leaning on Others 🤝

Who supports you when things get tough? How do they help?

5️⃣ Flexibility – Adapting to Change 🌊

How do you usually respond when your plans change unexpectedly?

6️⃣ Purpose – Knowing Your Why 🧭

What gives your life meaning and motivates you to keep going?

7️⃣ Self-Care – Refueling Your Mind & Body 🛁

List three things you do that help you feel calm, cared for, or restored.

🌈 Build Your Resilience Plan

Choose one trait above to focus on this week. How will you practice it?

“Resilience isn’t about never falling. It’s about rising stronger each time.” ✨
