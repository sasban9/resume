🧠 Resilience Workbook

What’s in My Control and What’s Not 🎛️🌈

🔍 Why This Matters

  • 🌦️ Life brings both challenges and opportunities.
  • 🛠️ Learning to focus on what we can control builds confidence and calm.
  • 🧘‍♀️ Accepting what we can’t control creates inner peace.

🎯 What Can I Control?

List 5 things you *can* control in your daily life.

🌪️ What Can’t I Control?

List 5 things that are *not* in your control but often affect you.

🛑 Letting Go of What’s Not Yours

Write about something that bothered you recently. What part of it was out of your control?

⚙️ Taking Charge

From that same situation, what could you control or do differently next time?

💪 Coping Toolbox

List 3 healthy coping strategies you use (or want to try) when things feel out of control.

🌤️ What Helps You Bounce Back?

Describe a tough situation you handled well. What helped you recover or grow stronger?

💬 Your Resilient Voice

Write an affirmation you can tell yourself when things feel overwhelming.

"Worrying doesn’t take away tomorrow’s troubles – it takes away today’s peace." 🕊️
