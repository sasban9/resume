💪 Resilience Workbook

Bounce Back Moments in My Life 🌈

🌧️ Tough Times I've Faced

Think of 1–2 challenges you’ve gone through. Write them down briefly:

🛠️ How Did I Cope?

What did you do to deal with these situations? What helped you feel better or stay strong?

💡 What Did I Learn About Myself?

Write 2–3 strengths or positive qualities you discovered in yourself during those times.

📦 My Coping Toolkit

List healthy ways you deal with stress, sadness, or frustration.

🧭 Resilience Reflection

Complete this sentence: “Resilience means...”

🌻 Message to My Future Self

Write a short message you’d like to read the next time you face a hard moment.

🦸‍♀️ Resilience Role Models

Who inspires you when it comes to bouncing back from tough situations? Why?
