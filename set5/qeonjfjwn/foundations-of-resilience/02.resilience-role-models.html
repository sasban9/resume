🌱 Coping & Resilience Workbook

My Resilience Role Models 💪

🧭 What's This About?

  • 🌟 Identifying people who inspire you through their resilience
  • 🧠 Exploring what makes someone emotionally strong
  • 💡 Learning to adopt helpful coping strategies from role models

👀 Who Inspires You?

List 2–3 people (real or fictional) who you think show great resilience.

🔍 What Makes Them Resilient?

Choose one of them. What have they overcome? How did they do it?

💬 Their Words or Actions

Write a quote or describe something they did that you admire.

🛠️ Coping Skills You Notice

What coping strategies does your role model use?

🪞 Reflection: What Can You Learn?

How can you apply any of their strengths or skills in your own life?

🌈 Your Strength Statement

Write a sentence about one strength you admire and want to develop in yourself.

"Resilience doesn’t mean never breaking—it means always finding a way to mend." 💖🧵
