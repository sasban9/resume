💥 Stress & Resilience Workbook

Understanding My Stress Response

🧠 What This Workbook Helps You With:

  • 💭 Recognizing your stress triggers
  • ⚡ Understanding how your body and mind react
  • 🛠️ Learning tools to build inner resilience

🌪️ What Does Stress Feel Like for You?

Describe how your body and mind feel when you're stressed.

🚨 What Triggers Your Stress?

Write down situations, people, or thoughts that usually trigger your stress.

🌀 Flight, Fight, or Freeze?

When you're under pressure, which response shows up first?

🛠️ Your Current Coping Tools

What are your go-to ways of coping? Are they helpful or harmful?

🌈 Healthy Coping Strategies

List 5 positive ways to manage your stress that you'd like to try more often.

💪 My Resilience Superpowers

Think about tough times you've overcome. What strengths helped you get through?

📦 Create Your Coping Kit

Design a personal 'stress survival kit' with items, habits, or reminders that help you feel grounded.

📅 Plan Your Calm

What’s one daily habit you can commit to that helps reduce stress?

💬 Your Resilience Affirmation

Write a personal reminder that you are stronger than you think.

"Stress is a signal, not a sentence. I choose how I respond." 🌊💛
