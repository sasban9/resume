💪 Resilience Workbook

Turning Setbacks Into Comebacks 🌈

🌟 What You'll Learn

  • 🧠 Understand your responses to setbacks
  • 🛠️ Explore healthy coping strategies
  • 🚀 Build resilience and bounce back stronger

🌀 What Set Me Back?

Describe a recent situation where you felt like you failed or got stuck.

🧭 How Did I React?

What was your first reaction? What emotions did you feel?

🧰 Coping Toolbox

List healthy ways you’ve coped in the past, or ones you'd like to try.

🔁 Reframing the Setback

What did you learn from this experience? How could this actually help you grow?

🧱 Building My Bounce-Back Plan

What can you do next time to cope more positively and stay motivated?

🎯 My Strengths That Help Me Rise

List 3 personal strengths you can lean on when life gets hard.

🛤️ Create a Comeback Motto

Write a powerful sentence that reminds you setbacks are stepping stones.

"Fall seven times, stand up eight." – Japanese Proverb 🌅💪
