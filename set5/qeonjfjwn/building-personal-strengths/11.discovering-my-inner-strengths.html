🛡️ Coping & Resilience Workbook

Discovering My Inner Strengths 🌟

💡 Why This Workbook?

  • 🌱 Build awareness of your natural coping tools
  • 🧠 Recognize how you handle challenges
  • 💪 Celebrate your personal strengths and resilience

🔍 What Do You Do When Things Get Tough?

Write down your usual ways of dealing with stress or hard times.

🧩 Identify Your Coping Strategies

Which of these do you use often? Add your own too!

  • 🎵 Listening to music
  • 📖 Reading or watching something calming
  • 💬 Talking to someone
  • 🎨 Drawing or expressing creatively
  • 🧘‍♂️ Breathing or mindfulness

💭 What Makes You Feel Strong?

Think about a time you felt proud of handling something hard. What helped you get through it?

⚡ Your Strength Superpowers

Write 3 strengths you believe you have when facing challenges.

🧱 Resilience in Action

Resilience is bouncing back after difficulties. Write one way you bounced back recently.

🌤️ Future You

Imagine yourself a year from now—stronger, wiser, more confident. What advice would that future version give you?

✨ My Strength Statement

Create your own empowering sentence to remind yourself of your resilience.

"You’ve survived 100% of your hardest days so far – that’s proof of your strength." 💖
