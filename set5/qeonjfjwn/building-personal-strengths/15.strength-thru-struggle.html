💪 Resilience Workbook

Strength Through Struggle – What I’ve Learned

🔍 What This Workbook Is About

  • 🧩 Recognizing difficult moments you've faced
  • 🛠️ Identifying the healthy tools you've used to cope
  • 🌱 Discovering the strength that came from your struggle

⛰️ My Tough Moment

Describe a difficult experience that challenged you emotionally or mentally.

🧠 What Did I Feel?

Reflect on the emotions you experienced during that time.

🛠️ My Coping Tools

What helped you get through it? (People, habits, mindset shifts...)

🌟 What Did I Learn?

Think of the wisdom or strength you gained from the experience.

🤗 Who Was There For Me?

Was there someone who supported you? How did they show up for you?

🧘‍♀️ Healthy vs. Unhealthy Coping

Make a list of what helps you feel better in a healthy way, and what doesn't.

🧩 My Resilience Superpowers

What personal strengths did you discover in yourself through this struggle?

📖 My Strength Statement

Write a sentence or paragraph that captures how you’ve grown through struggle.

"Rocks in my path? I keep them all. With them, I build my castle." 🏰💎
